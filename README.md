## Features

- **Upgrade Tracking** - Track all your village upgrades in one place
- **Widget Support** - View your upgrades directly from your home screen
- **Data Import** - Easily import your upgrade data from Game Press

## How to Use

### Step 1: Export Data from Game Press

1. Open the **Clash of Clans Game Press** website on your device
2. Navigate to your player profile
3. Find the **Copy** button on the page
4. Click the Copy button to copy your player JSON data to clipboard

### Step 2: Import Data into the App

1. Open the **COC Upgrade Manager** app on your Android device
2. Look for the **Process** button in the app
3. The app will automatically read the JSON data from your clipboard
4. Your upgrades will be imported and displayed in the app

### Step 3: Using Widgets

The app provides several home screen widgets for quick access to your upgrade information:

- **List Widget** - Shows a list of all your pending upgrades
- **Stats Widget** - Displays upgrade statistics and progress
- **Upgrade Widget** - Shows the next upgrade in queue

To add a widget:
1. Long press on your home screen
2. Select "Widgets" from the menu
3. Find "COC Upgrade Manager" in the list
4. Choose the widget type you want to add
5. Position the widget on your home screen
